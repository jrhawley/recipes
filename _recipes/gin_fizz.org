#+TITLE: Gin fizz
#+DATE: [2020-12-19]
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2022-07-25 Mon 09:04]
#+FILETAGS: :recipe:alcoholic:beverage:

| Info      | Amount     |
|-----------+------------|
| Prep Time | 2 min      |
| Yields    | 1 cocktail |

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item         |
|----------+--------------|
| 1.5 oz   | [[../_ingredients/gin.md][gin]]          |
| 1 oz     | [[./simple-syrup.md][simple syrup]] |
| 1 oz     | [[../_ingredients/lime-juice.md][lime juice]]   |
|          | [[../_ingredients/ice.md][ice cubes]]    |
| 4 oz     | [[id:dba4890c-1a4a-44b8-8ab2-c6414d166052][club soda]]    |

* Directions

1. Place all ingredients but the club soda into a cocktail shaker.
2. Cover and shake vigorously
3. Strain into highball glasses, then add in club soda
