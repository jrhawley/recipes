#+TITLE: Basil and tomato orzo
#+DATE: [2017-05-28]
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2023-04-02 Sun 18:50]
#+FILETAGS: :pasta:recipes:vegetarian:lunch:entree:

| Info      | Amount     |
|-----------+------------|
| Prep Time | 15 min     |
| Cook Time | 10 min     |
| Yields    | 4 servings |

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item                     |
|----------+--------------------------|
| 2 cups   | [[id:c3b39e8a-9df8-4f90-999e-7f888278d52e][orzo]], uncooked           |
| 25       | fresh [[id:f62c8021-74a6-4070-a240-25e5c072cdba][basil]] leaves       |
| 1 Tbsp   | [[id:88239f38-3c15-4b0d-8052-54718aaea7a3][oregano]] leaves           |
| 1        | [[id:7b7e13cf-0fb9-4dc6-a707-94bfd73417b2][shallot]]                  |
| 1 clove  | [[id:f120187f-f080-4f7c-b2cc-72dc56228a07][garlic]], minced           |
| 1/3 cup  | [[id:a3cbe672-676d-4ce9-b3d5-2ab7cdef6810][olive oil]]                |
| 1 tsp    | [[id:257897fc-30ec-4477-aa93-abff6398d8c1][honey]]                    |
| 1 Tbsp   | [[id:970d7f49-6f00-4caf-b73f-90d3e7f5039c][water]]                    |
| 1 cup    | [[id:32d73adc-34f4-4ff8-ace7-e19dbd9905aa][cherry tomatoes]], halved  |
| 1 cup    | [[id:a2ed6c9e-2e2c-4918-b61b-78c3c9d36c8c][parmesan]], grated         |
| 5        | [[id:03c1357c-225f-455d-af1f-86c11d3eafb9][mint]] leaves, chopped     |
| 1        | [[id:3bf1d509-27e0-42f6-a975-be224e071ba7][lemon]], zested and juiced |
|          | [[id:026747d6-33c9-43c8-9d71-e201ed476116][kosher salt]]              |

* Directions

1. Cook orzo pasta according to package directions. Set aside.
2. Place basil leaves, shallot, garlic, oil, lemon zest and juice, honey and salt in a blender with water and give it a whiz until blended.
3. Mix dressing into pasta
   - Stir in cherry tomatoes and Parmesan
   - Garnish with fresh chopped mint
