#+TITLE: Banana bread
#+DATE: [2013-09-21]
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2022-07-25 Mon 08:45]
#+FILETAGS: :recipe:

| Info      | Amount |
|-----------+--------|
| Prep Time | 10 min |
| Cook Time | 1 h    |
| Yields    | 1 loaf |

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item             |
|----------+------------------|
| 1/4 cup  | [[id:c2560014-7e89-4ef5-a628-378773b307e5][butter]]           |
| 1/4 cup  | [[../_ingredients/sugar.md][sugar]]            |
| 1        | [[../_ingredients/egg.md][egg]], beaten      |
| 2        | [[../_ingredients/banana.md][bananas]], crushed |
| 3/2 cup  | [[id:52b06361-3a75-4b35-84ff-6b1f3ac96b23][flour]]            |
| 1 tsp    | [[../_ingredients/baking-soda.md][baking soda]]      |
| 1/2 tsp  | [[id:505e3767-00ab-4806-8966-555302b06297][table salt]]       |
| 1/2 tsp  | [[../_ingredients/vanilla-extract.md][vanilla]]          |

* Directions

1. Cream together butter and sugar
   - Add eggs and crushed bananas
2. Sift together flour, soda and salt
   - Add to creamed mixture
   - Add vanilla
3. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan
4. Bake at 350 F for 1h
