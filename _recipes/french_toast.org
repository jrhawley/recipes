|-----------+------------|

| Yields    | 4 servings |
#+DATE: [2013-09-14]

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item                                             |
|----------+--------------------------------------------------|
| 2        | [[../_ingredients/cherry-tomato.md][eggs]]       |
| 2/3 cup  | [[../_ingredients/milk.md][milk]]                |
| 2 tsp    | ground [[../_ingredients/cinnamon.md][cinnamon]] |
| 8 slices | [[../_ingredients/bread.md][bread]]              |
| 1 tsp    | [[../_ingredients/butter.md][butter]], melted    |

* Directions

1. Either melt butter in frying pan/griddle or melt and mix with other ingredients
2. Beat to make the mixture light
3. Dip bread and fry
4. Top with berries, jam, icing sugar, etc
