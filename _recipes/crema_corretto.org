#+TITLE: Crema corretto
#+DATE: [2021-09-25]
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2022-09-27 Tue 09:37]
#+FILETAGS: :recipe:alcohol:beverage:

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item             |
|----------+------------------|
| 2 oz     | [[id:f0c2d0c7-8399-45f1-a783-1be5ca83aafd][amaro]]            |
| 1 oz     | [[id:eaf54217-f528-4d72-b1b1-4efff76983ae][whipping cream]]   |
| 3/4 oz   | [[id:5af07580-c639-47a4-86af-1003c1635152][espresso]]         |
| 2        | [[id:b3692180-16ba-453c-ac93-9d3c5787cecc][chocolate sticks]] |

* Directions

1. Shake amaro, cream, and espresso over ice until cold.
2. Strain into an ice-filled rocks glass and garnish with chocolate sticks.
