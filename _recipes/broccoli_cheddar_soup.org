#+TITLE: Broccoli and cheddar soup
#+DATE: [2019-11-13]
#+LAST_MODIFIED: [2023-04-02 Sun 18:40]
#+FILETAGS: :soups:recipes:vegetarian:entree:

| Info      | Amount     |
|-----------+------------|
| Prep Time | 10 min     |
| Cook Time | 3 h        |
| Yields    | 6 servings |

* Ingredients

| Quantity | Item                            |
|----------+---------------------------------|
| 5 cups   | [[id:dfedd038-479e-42e5-9995-bd0beeea0dd4][broccoli]] florets, thinly sliced |
| 1        | medium [[id:8a695016-03b5-4059-9a54-668f3b794e33][yellow onion]], diced      |
| 3 cloves | [[id:f120187f-f080-4f7c-b2cc-72dc56228a07][garlic]], minced                  |
| 1 cup    | [[id:7fc6b423-7144-4755-ab42-4c7886d3069d][carrots]], grated                 |
| 2 oz     | reduced fat [[id:d208148d-02fe-4a3d-8675-f4934bc5a298][cream cheese]]        |
| 1 tsp    | [[id:505e3767-00ab-4806-8966-555302b06297][table salt]]                      |
| 1 tsp    | [[id:68516e6c-ad08-45fd-852b-ba45ce50a68b][pepper]]                          |
| 1/4 tsp  | [[id:cfdf3e4e-72c5-4bd2-aa76-dc03d92305ca][nutmeg]]                          |
| 4 cups   | [[id:6aaa4d74-e28e-4e22-afc6-dc6cf0dee4ac][vegetable broth]]                 |
| 1 Tbsp   | [[id:970d7f49-6f00-4caf-b73f-90d3e7f5039c][water]]                           |
| 1/4 cup  | warm [[id:970d7f49-6f00-4caf-b73f-90d3e7f5039c][water]]                      |
| 1 Tbsp   | [[id:74ca1bf4-e5d8-4e1c-89fe-fb8a82ad8249][corn starch]]                     |
| 1/4 cup  | plain [[id:3e80ceb1-aa3e-425a-a18b-d3acdc4353cf][Greek yogurt]]              |
| 1 cup    | [[id:a89a233e-066a-40bd-8081-de30271f7576][cheddar]], grated                 |

* Directions

1. Add vegetable broth, broccoli, onion, garlic, carrots, and cream cheese to slow cooker
   1. Stir to combine
   2. Cook on low for 4-5 h or high for 2-3 h
2. When finished, mix 1 Tbsp of water with cornstarch in a saucepan
   1. Add Greek yogurt and warm water
   2. Simmer on low, add cheddar cheese until creamy and melted
   3. Whisk into slow cooker
